Four Seasons of Yuzawa

On the island country of Japan, a small village is nestled among the wild, northern mountains.

Winter

雪

As winter approaches, the people of Yuzawa board up windows and build shelters for their delicate trees. Known for its tremendous amount of snowfall, Yuzawa is blanketed by deep drifts of soft, white snow every winter. Windows
